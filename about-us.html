About Us

We're two guys located in San Francisco and Los Angeles, that follow the indie game scene quite closely. We've struggled to find a website that presented in-depth, quality reviews so we decided to strike out and do it ourselves. Our reviews are a candid unbiased view and revolve around one question, "Did we have fun playing it?". Our reviews are chockful of handcrafted, high-quality gifs, so you can get a better feel for the game. If you're interested in us reviewing your game, or have general feedback on the site, drop us a line at: indiesensereviews [at] gmail.com

Thanks for taking the time to visit our site.

- Brian Ta and Joseph Pham
